When a geographic area reaches a certain size It should be broken into smaller pieces. Jurisdictional lines should be redrawn and new local government should be put in place for each locality. 

Baltimore City, a place I am familiar with, is a prime example of the need for this break up. North, South, East, and West Baltimore are all vastly different from each other demographically and geographically. The needs of the people of North Baltimore are different than the people of the East. North Baltimore is crime and poverty stricken. It's needs are largely ignored and it's people have no real influence in City government. Yet the City funds itself from the taxes it levies against those very people it is supposed to be serving. 

If North Baltimore were to break off from City Hall and start it's own City Hall, it's new mayor and City Council would be made up of people exclusively from their new City and thus share the same concerns as everyone else. This means funds would be less likely to be siphoned off for less important projects.
