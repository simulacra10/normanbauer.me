>What is needed is an electronic payment system based on cryptographic proof instead of trust,
>allowing any two willing parties to transact directly with each other without the need for a trusted
>third party. Transactions that are computationally impractical to reverse would protect sellers
>from fraud, and routine escrow mechanisms could easily be implemented to protect buyers.
>Satoshi Nakamoto - Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System aka The Bitcoin Whitepaper

There are a handful of [[Books]] that I can point to in my life that I have read that have radically changed the way I look at everything. One of them was _What Has Government Done To Our Money? - By Murray N. Rothbard_ . When I read it I could see as clear as day that quite possibly the #1 reason The United States is in the condition it is in presently was due to having a monetary policy that is a plague on civilization. Rothbard proposes in the book that it is because the Federal Reserve and Treasury can create money out of thin air, that allows government to grow into the beast that it has become. Rothbard's solution is to return to a Gold Standard. Gold being limited in supply, as the theory goes, would limit the ability or at least temper the ability of a government to unleash the printing press (printer go brrrrrr!).

Once your read that, you are eventually also going to end up reading the expanded argument that won Hayek a Nobel Laureate:  _The Denationalization of Money: The Argument Refined - By Friedrich Hayek_  This is where Hayek argues that it is private competing currencies that would actually solve the woes of humanity that government has plagued us with.  Hayek proposes that government has carved out an exclusive right to control the money supply, yet has several thousand years of history proving that it is completely unworthy of the task. In 1974, he could have not have even dreamed of what it now possible and just how awesome his proposal would be. Thus after reading the _Bitcoin Whitepaper_ I knew exactly where Sitoshi got his inspiration from, Hayek! 

Fast forward to about two years ago. Someone close to me. Someone in my inner circle mentions to me that a new technology called QFS - The Quantum Financial System, is being created to "take down the global cabal". Remember those words because it is the catch phrase that every single person who talks about QFS uses it.

When you use that phrase with me, you actually get my attention. Why? Because I know there is such a thing. I can point you to them. The World Economic Forum, The United Nations, The Financial Action Task Force, and dozens of other International NGO's and Think Tanks, who wield power and influence. They are very real and very much a risk to freedom and Free Market Capitalism. They are the best friend of Crony Capitalism, Fascism, Socialism, and top down governance. They are the enemies of freedom. 

Below is a description of QFS and it's proposed purpose:

>"QFS is Quantum Financial System an Advanced Financial System Launched to Eradicate the Cabal and Banksters Monopoly on Monetary System and for that Purpose, a system comprises of Artificial Intelligence and complex computer programs fully backed by banks is needed." - [@EnlightendNow7 on Twitter 6/2/2020](https://twitter.com/EnlightenedNow7/status/1267947574809542656)

While I have zero reason to believe that QFS is anything more than a work of fiction, I think the best path to understanding why you should not put any money into this is to treat it as if it were _true_.
